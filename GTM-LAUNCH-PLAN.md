## 1. Product Positioning

### One-Liner
> "Automated QA for vibe coders — find bugs before your users do."

### Value Proposition
You ship fast with Cursor/Bolt/v0. But bugs still slip through. VibeQA scans your site in minutes and catches what you missed — broken links, mobile issues, console errors, and more. No signup required for your first test.

### Target Customer Profile
**Primary:** Vibe coders using Cursor, Bolt.new, v0.dev, Replit who ship MVPs fast but don't have time for manual QA.

**Secondary:** 
- Indie hackers launching SaaS products
- Small agencies QA'ing client sites before handoff
- No-code builders (Bubble, Webflow, Framer)

### Why Us vs. Alternatives
| Competitor | Price | Problem |
|------------|-------|---------|
| UserTesting | $49+/video | Too expensive, too slow |
| Lighthouse | Free | Generic, no context |
| Manual testing | $0 | Time sink, not repeatable |
| **VibeQA** | **Free tier / $29/mo** | **Fast, automated, built for indie devs** |

## 5. Pricing Strategy

### Current Pricing (vibeqa.io)
| Tier | Price | Limits |
|------|-------|--------|
| Free | $0 | 1 scan/day, 7-day history |
| Pro | $29/mo | 100 scans, AI analysis, API |
| Team | $79/mo | Unlimited, 10 members |

### Launch Week Strategy
1. **Free tier is the hook** — no signup for first scan removes all friction
2. **Don't discount** — price anchoring matters; $29 is already cheap
3. **Annual option?** — consider $249/year (2 months free) for Pro

### When to Adjust
- If conversion is low: Add a $9/mo "Starter" tier (20 scans)
- If free users don't convert: Add watermark or limit report depth
- If agencies ask: Add $199/mo white-label option
